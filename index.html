Studio Ghibli

It produces anime shows and movies. It has won many awards for some of its movies, including an Academy Award for Spirited Away. The company was established by Hayao Miyazaki in 1985. The name comes from the Arabic word ghibli, for hot winds blowing from the Sahara Desert.

About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli was founded on June 15, 1985, by the directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata and producer Toshio Suzuki, after acquiring Topcraft's assets. The studio has also collaborated with video game studios on the visual development of several games.Studio Ghibli, Inc. is a Japanese animation studio based in Koganei, Tokyo. It has a strong presence in the animation industry and has expanded its portfolio to include various media formats, such as short subjects, television commercials, and two television films.

The Boy and the Heron

The Boy and the Heron was recently released on december 8, 2023.

This film is about a boy named Mahito, a young 12-year-old boy, struggles to settle in a new town after his mother's death. However, when a talking heron informs Mahito that his mother is still alive, he enters an abandoned tower in search of her, which takes him to another world.

After capturing hearts and soaring to success at the box office, Hayao Miyazaki's acclaimed film "The Boy and the Heron" is set to make a triumphant return to theaters following its recent Oscar win. With its enchanting storyline and breathtaking animation, the movie has captivated audiences worldwide, solidifying its place as a beloved classic in the realm of animated cinema.

Something special and magical about Studio Ghibli

A huge selling point of Studio Ghibli films is their romanticised representations of everyday life. The audience can see Japanese food, customs, and locations all beautifully visualised.

Their stunning animation ! One of the first things that immediately stands out when watching any Studio Ghibli film is the hand-drawn animation that's become a trademark of their productions.
